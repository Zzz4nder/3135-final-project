My Study Abroad Experience

For my study abroad trip I went to Pairs, France and Dubrovnik, Croatia during the 2024 spring break. The trip was around ten days total. Of the ten, two consisted of traveling the entire day. For more infomation on either city, see below and to see cultural differences click on either city name.

Paris, France

In Paris I spent around four days. Classes started at 11:00 am and ended at 4:00 pm so there was a good amount of time to do things in the city especially since most places close around 2:00 am. Around the popular tourist locations practically all people spoke english although the further you get away, from those locations the locals tend to only speak french.

Dubrovnik, Croatia

In Dubrovnik I also spent around four days. Dubrovnik is a small Coastal city and my trip was during an off season for tourism as the Town was cold. For this reason there wasn't as much to do as in Paris. Also, many places tended to close earlier than Paris, around 9:00 pm. The majority of locals spoke English but less so than in Paris. The beauty of the city consisted of the surrounding landscape and the architecture.
