Welcome!

Hello welcome to my Guide to Study Abroad at UNCC! Here you can find how the requirements/where to start if you want to Study Abroad, My Experience studying aborad and where to get some culture expereinces without leaving our campus!

For the past two years, I have been a member of the Global Engagement Scholars Program. I joined the GESP to learn more about different cultures and gain better cultural awareness including a deeper understanding of the world. Throughout my journey in the GESP, I have developed a wide intercultural understanding through the three different components of this program which includes an international academic course, co-curricular activities, and an international engagement experience. For the academic course component, I learned more in-depth about global social science and anthropology. For the co-curricular activities segment, I participated in various international events at UNC Charlotte. For the international engagement experience, I studied abroad in France and Croatia.

About Me

My name is Natalie Torres. I am a senior at UNC Charlotte majoring in Marketing and minoring in International Management. My passions are music, meeting new people, traveling, trying new food, and volunteering in the community.

This website will provide insight into my cultural experiences both abroad and at UNC Charlotte. I will cover my experince abroad in Paris, France and Dubrovnik, Croatia. Lastly how you can study abroad and experince culturals here at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte.

I hope this site can inspire others to consider studying abroad and explore other cultures. I believe it is very important to be open-minded and accepting of cultural differences.
